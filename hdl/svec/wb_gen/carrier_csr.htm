RESERVED[2:0] DDR1_CAL_DONE DDR0_CAL_DONE SYS_PLL_LCK FMC1_PRES FMC0_PRES
  • FMC0_PRES [read-only]: FMC 1 presence
    0: FMC slot 1 is populated
    1: FMC slot 1 is not populated.
  • FMC1_PRES [read-only]: FMC 2 presence
    0: FMC slot 2 is populated
    1: FMC slot 2 is not populated.
  • SYS_PLL_LCK [read-only]: System clock PLL status
    0: not locked
    1: locked.
  • DDR0_CAL_DONE [read-only]: DDR3 bank 4 calibration status
    0: not done
    1: done.
  • DDR1_CAL_DONE [read-only]: DDR3 bank 5 calibration status
    0: not done
    1: done.
  • RESERVED [read-only]: Reserved
    Ignore on read, write with 0's.
